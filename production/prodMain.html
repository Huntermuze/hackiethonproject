You sit down at the computer, and you swear you'll be productive. Next thing you know, it's twelve hours later. You've checked your email, updated your Facebook status, blown through 200 pages of Reddit, read every article in your Twitter feed, looked up your favorite band on Wikipedia, vanity googled yourself, cyber-stalked your ex, browsed through all your high-school crushes' Instagram feeds, and lost a week's pay playing online poker.


What you haven't done is WORK.


WorkMate's productivity mode was designed with the concepts of efficiency and effectiveness at the forefront of our minds. Each feature embedded within the cards below serves to aid your working from home experience, ensuring that distractions are kept as far away as possible. This way, you'll be able to get a lot more out of your day and complete your work sooner, rather than by next Christmas.

TIPS: If any of the below feature cards seem useful to you, pin it by simply clicking the card's title. This way, you'll know what's enabled and what's disabled!
Productivity Timer

Description

First begin by inputting your amount of focus time (in minutes) and break time (in minutes), then save the setting. Then select "start focus" to begin your period of solid productivity. You can pause this period inbetween to account for any unexpected interruptions, or you can reset if need be.


Click the "start break" to reap the benefits of your well-deserved break!
